CET Certification

The 6-unit CET Concentration in Engineering Leadership empowers students to become technology leaders through classroom and hands-on study of entrepreneurship and commercialization. Students not pursuing the concentration are welcome to enroll in individual classes.

The concentration includes any 6 units of the related coursework found below:
  • Real Life Introduction to Entrepreneurship & Innovation:
    • Distinguished Innovator Lecture Series (E 198) / 1 unit, P/NP
  • Multidisciplinary Project Courses:
    • Engineering Entrepreneurship (IEOR 191) / 3 units
    • Technology Leadership (IEOR 171) / 3 units
    • Berkeley Method of Entrepreneurship-Bootcamp and class (IEOR 190A) / 2 or 3 units Web 2.0 (IEOR 190C) /1 or 2 units
    • Mobile Applications/Product Management (IEOR 190D) 2 units
    • Mobile Applications (IEOR 190E) / 3 units
    • B.O.A.T. Business Opportunities in Art & Technology (IEOR 190F) / 1 or 2 units Patent Engineering (IEOR 190G) / 1 or 2 units
    • Finance (E 120) / 1 unit
  • Other approved elective activities that apply towards the concentration:
    • LeaderShape Institute program may use that experience for one (1) elective credit.
    • FounderSchool may use that experience for one (1) elective credit.
    • Berkeley Mobile International Collaborative (BMIC) Challenge may use that experience for one (1) elective credit. Participants in the CET Venture Lab Competition may use that experience for credit.
    • Participants in Skydeck Start-Up may use that experience for 1 or 2 credits (dependent on contribution.)
How to apply

Please submit the online application during the semester in which you will complete the concentration requirements. If you have questions, please email Marcia Hickman, CET Student Services Officer (marciah@berkeley.edu).
